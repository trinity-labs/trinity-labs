# Pocket Mainframe
*(aka Home Mainframe)*
<br>
**Begin of the story** - Re-use a screen broken laptop motherboard to get an home server, home theater and small Nas. Board from **TOSHIBA P875-101**
- 4 DDR3 Up to 32Go
- Intel Core i7 3610QM - 4 Cores | 8 Threads - TDP 45W - *No fanless yet but semi-passive*
- NVIDIA GeForce GT 640M
- 3 SATA III (convert optical drive)
- 1 HDMI
- 1 LAN
- 1 LVDS
- 2 USB3
- Battery Removed - Low powered @ 90W power supply
